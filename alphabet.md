# Alphabet

Rules of alphabet:

Vouls stand alone:
Consonants are terminated with 'o' or 'u':

```
-------------------------------------------
A   B   C   D   E   F   G   H   I   J   
a   bo  cu  do  e   fu  go  hu  i   jo
-------------------------------------------
L   M   N   O   P   R   S   T   U   V   Z
lu  mo  nu  o   pu  ro  su  to  u   vo  zu
-------------------------------------------
```
Not used: X Q K W Y 

* Vocals  : a  e  i  o  u
* Digraph : ts,   sh 
* Trigraph: tce,  tci

## Logic

* Afirmativ  : da   = yes
* Negativ    : no   = not

* Interogativ: tce  = what is it
* Interogativ: tci  = who is it
