# Symbols

Maj is using next symbols for mathematical expressions and text:

Symbol | Maj  | English
-------|------|---------------------
`x	`  | xai	| Multiply (times)
`\	`  | oba	| Backward slash
`()	`  | bra	| Round brackets
`:	`  | epa	| Colon
`;	`  | iva	| Semicolon
`_	`  | spa	| blank, gap
`>	`  | gwo	| Greater then sign
`=	`  | ego	| Equal sign
`â‰ 	`  | ugo	| Inequal sign
`!	`  | ira	| Exclamation mark
`?	`  | kwa	| Question mark
`<	`  | lwo	| Less then sign
`-	`  | fio	| Hyphen , minus sign
`.	`  | bla	| Dot, full stop
`,	`  | oga	| Comma
`+	`  | eth	| Plus sign
`[]	`  | gra	| Square brackets
`*	`  | sta	| Asterisk
`/	`  | osa	| Slash , forward slash
`{}	`  | sra	| Curly brackets

**notes:**

Symbols are nouns so they end with "a". We will learn later this can be changed in "e" to make plural. 

un ipa = one dot
du ipe = two dots
