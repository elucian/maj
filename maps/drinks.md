## Most frequent drinks.

* All alcoholic drinks are terminated with "ol"
* If a root word is too short we use "am" or "om"
* Some drinks have a form of plural that means something else:

acue  = waters (river and lakes)
late  = dairy products
fesce = soft drinks

**related verb**

To drink: beba-go
